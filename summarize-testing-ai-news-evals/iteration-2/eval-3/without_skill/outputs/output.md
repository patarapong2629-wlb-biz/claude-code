## Broader Context

The period is defined by a tension: AI is generating code faster than testing can keep up with, and most organizations are still in early maturity when it comes to AI-assisted QA. The emerging response is agentic testing platforms that can autonomously generate, run, and maintain tests — but these introduce new questions about trust, reliability, and governance.

The "testing AI" problem (evaluating AI models themselves) is now being treated as a distinct discipline from "AI-assisted testing" (using AI tools to test software), and both are attracting investment and standards activity.
